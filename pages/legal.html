Data Protection

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1 January 2026

Golden Investment Association (GIA) is committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of its members and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to your data. GIA operates in compliance with applicable data protection principles under Ugandan law.

1. Information We Collect

GIA collects personal information only to the extent necessary to administer membership and association operations. This may include:

  • Full name, date of birth, and national identification number
  • Contact information (phone number, email address, physical address)
  • Employment and income information
  • Banking and payment details for contributions and withdrawals
  • Emergency contact details
  • Records of savings, loans, and investment participation

2. How We Use Your Information

Personal information collected is used exclusively for:

  • Administering membership accounts and maintaining membership records
  • Processing savings contributions and managing withdrawals
  • Managing loan applications, disbursement, and repayment tracking
  • Facilitating investment participation and tracking returns
  • Communicating important notices and updates to members
  • Financial reporting and auditing purposes
  • Complying with legal obligations under Ugandan law

3. Confidentiality

Data Protection: GIA maintains strict confidentiality of member information. Personal data is stored securely and accessible only to authorized GIA officials and necessary service providers.

Sharing of Information: GIA does not share member information with external parties without explicit consent, except when required by law or for legitimate business purposes such as external audits. All third parties are bound by confidentiality obligations.

Member Records: Members have the right to access their own records held by GIA. Requests should be directed to the GIA Secretary.

4. Data Retention

GIA retains member information for as long as the membership is active and for a reasonable period afterward for financial, legal, and auditing purposes. Information is not used for purposes other than those specified in this policy.

5. Your Rights

Members have the following rights regarding their personal data:

  • The right to access information held about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate information
  • The right to withdraw consent for data processing
  • The right to lodge a complaint if you believe your privacy has been violated

GIA may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be communicated to members, and continued membership constitutes acceptance of updated terms.

Investment Disclaimer

Effective Date: 1 January 2026

IMPORTANT NOTICE: This disclaimer outlines the risks and important considerations associated with participation in Golden Investment Association (GIA) group investments.

1. No Guarantee of Returns

GIA does not guarantee any returns on member savings, contributions, or investments. Past investment performance does not indicate or guarantee future results. The value of investments may go up or down, and members may lose capital.

2. Risk Acknowledgment

By becoming a member and participating in GIA investments, you acknowledge that:

  • All investments carry inherent risk of loss
  • Economic, market, and operational factors may affect investment performance
  • Investment decisions made by the General Assembly are binding on all participating members
  • Losses from investments are shared collectively among members
  • No member shall hold GIA liable for investment losses beyond their contributed capital

3. Regulatory Status

GIA is a voluntary member-owned group and is not regulated by financial authorities as a bank or investment fund. Members do not benefit from government deposit protection schemes or investment guarantees that apply to licensed financial institutions.

4. Investment Decisions

All investment decisions are made through democratic vote by the General Assembly and require a two-thirds (2/3) majority. Members participating in investments accept that:

  • They have thoroughly understood the investment proposal
  • They accept the full risk of loss associated with that investment
  • They agree to be bound by the outcome of the investment

5. Financial Literacy

Members are encouraged to participate in financial literacy workshops and seek understanding of investment terms and risks before voting on investment proposals. GIA cannot guarantee that all members fully understand the risks of every investment.

6. Lending Risks

GIA facilitates lending among members, but cannot guarantee that borrowers will repay loans as agreed. If a borrower defaults, the group's collective savings may be affected. Members accept this collective lending risk as part of membership.

7. Management Risks

While GIA maintains transparent operations and independent audits, management risks exist. Members acknowledge that:

  • Officer error or oversight, though unlikely, is possible
  • Operational disruptions could affect access to funds
  • External fraud or theft, despite safeguards, cannot be entirely ruled out

8. Withdrawal and Liquidity Risks

Not all of a member's savings may be immediately accessible. Early withdrawal penalties may apply, and liquidity may be limited during certain phases of the investment cycle. Members understand they may face delays in accessing their capital.

9. No Professional Investment Advice

GIA and its officers do not provide professional investment advice. Members are responsible for their own investment decisions and are encouraged to seek professional financial advice if needed.

10. Suitability

GIA is not suitable for all investors. It is intended for members willing to accept significant risk in exchange for potential long-term growth. If you cannot afford to lose your investment, GIA membership may not be appropriate for you.

11. Dispute Resolution

In case of disputes regarding investments or any GIA operations, members agree to attempt resolution through the established internal mechanisms of GIA before pursuing external legal action.

12. Limitation of Liability

GIA and its officers shall not be held liable for investment losses beyond the scope of their intentional misconduct or gross negligence. Members assume full responsibility for their participation in GIA investments.

13. No License or Registration Claim

GIA does not claim to be licensed, registered, or regulated by any financial authority. Membership in GIA is not equivalent to holding a regulated investment product.

14. Acknowledgment of Risk

By becoming a member or participating in GIA investments, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and accepted all risks outlined in this disclaimer.

Questions? If you have questions about any risks or disclaimers, please contact the GIA Secretary before making a membership decision.
