Getting Started

How to Join GIA

Our straightforward process makes it easy to become a member and start building wealth with us.

1

Submit Application

Complete a membership application form with your personal and financial information. We require basic details to understand your financial goals.

2

Meet Requirements

Ensure you meet basic eligibility criteria: be 18+, have valid identification, and demonstrate commitment to regular savings.

3

Pay Membership Fee

Pay a one-time membership fee (amount determined by the General Assembly). This fee covers registration and administrative costs.

4

Begin Saving

Start making regular monthly savings contributions. Your commitment to consistent savings is the foundation of your success.

Responsibilities

Member Obligations

As a GIA member, you agree to uphold the standards that make our association strong:

💵

Regular Contributions

Make consistent monthly savings contributions as agreed at General Assembly meetings.

📋

Follow By-Laws

Adhere to the GIA Constitution and By-Laws that govern our operations and member conduct.

🤝

Participate Actively

Attend meetings, vote on important matters, and contribute to group decisions.

⚖️

Repay Loans

If you borrow from the group, repay according to agreed terms and conditions.

🔐

Maintain Honesty

Provide accurate information and maintain integrity in all dealings with the group.

💬

Communicate

Keep informed about meetings, notices, and important announcements from GIA leadership.

Advantages

What You Get as a Member

Financial Products

Savings and Loan Details

Savings Account

Your monthly contributions are held collectively for maximum benefit. Withdrawals can be made at the end of agreed savings cycles.

Early Withdrawal: Members may withdraw up to 60% of total savings before the cycle ends, after settling any outstanding loans, subject to Executive Committee approval.

Loans

Members become eligible for loans once they have established a savings history. Loan limits, interest rates, and terms are determined by member consensus.

Important: Default on loans may result in recovery from your savings or guarantor's savings, and potential suspension from the group.
Growth Opportunities

Group Investments

GIA facilitates group investment decisions to help members' capital grow beyond traditional savings. All investment decisions are made collectively and require a two-thirds (2/3) majority vote.

Democratic Process

All members discuss and vote on investment opportunities. No single member's funds are committed without their explicit support.

Transparent Management

All investment activities are recorded, monitored, and reported regularly to members through financial statements and meetings.

Risk Awareness

We educate members on investment risks. Past returns do not guarantee future performance, and capital is never guaranteed.

Disclaimer: All investments carry risk. GIA cannot guarantee profit or return of capital from investment activities. Member decisions are binding, and losses are shared collectively.
