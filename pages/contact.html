Help

Frequently Asked Questions

How much is the membership fee?

The membership fee amount is set by member consensus at General Assembly meetings. Contact us for current rates.

What are the minimum savings contributions?

Monthly savings amounts are set by the membership and may vary. These are determined democratically to ensure accessibility for all members.

Can I withdraw my savings anytime?

Members can withdraw savings at the end of agreed savings cycles. Early withdrawals may receive up to 60% of total savings after settling loans.

What loan options are available?

Loan eligibility, limits, interest rates, and repayment terms are determined by the membership. Loans must be used responsibly and repaid as agreed.

How are investment decisions made?

All members discuss and vote on investment opportunities. Decisions require a two-thirds (2/3) majority vote from the General Assembly.

What happens if I miss a savings contribution?

Regular contributions are required to maintain membership. Persistent non-payment may result in discussions about your membership status.

Is my money safe with GIA?

GIA is governed by transparent practices, democratic oversight, and independent audits. An External Auditor reviews our financial records regularly.

How often do meetings occur?

General Assembly meetings are held monthly on scheduled dates. Emergency meetings can be called if needed to address urgent matters.
